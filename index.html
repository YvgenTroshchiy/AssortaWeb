How it works

You add. AI organizes. You find.

No folders to create, no tags to manage, no cleanup day. The sorting you always postpone is the part that happens on its own.

Drop it in

Paste text, or share straight from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram or your browser — Assorta lives in your share sheet.

AI files it

Assorta reads what it is and puts it in the right group: Travel, Cooking, Wish list, Movies, Health — 30+ groups, zero setup.

Find it fast

One colorful dashboard with rich link previews. Open the group, and it's exactly where you'd expect it to be.

Features

Built for the two seconds you're willing to spend

Saving something should cost nothing. Finding it should cost even less.

Share-sheet saving

Assorta sits in the system share menu on Android and iOS. Two taps and it's saved — you never leave the app you're in.

30+ smart groups

AI picks the group the moment you save. Disagree? Change it with one tap — right from the confirmation.

Link previews

Saved links keep their thumbnail and title — a YouTube frame, a product shot, an article cover — so you recognize them at a glance.

Saves in the background

Switch away mid-save — the item still lands safely in its group. Nothing gets lost between apps.

Everywhere you are

Android, iOS and the web. Save on your phone, find it on your laptop.

Yours to look at

Light and dark themes. English, Ukrainian, Russian, Spanish, French and German.
